Market Simulator



Hello Dear Readers,

I am glad to share that I have published my first big project in C#, called "Market Simulator," on GitHub.

This program includes 9 different sections and 145 products, just like in a real market, with their respective prices.

The program allows users to shop according to their preferences, and once they have finished shopping, they are given the option to pay either by cash or credit card. They can also choose to split their payment into installments, just like they would with a real credit card.

Once the payment process is completed, a message confirming the end of the program is displayed on the screen, and the program is closed.

I have tried to explain the workings of this program in a simple way. For those who wish to explore the program in more detail, I have provided a link to its GitHub repository below.
